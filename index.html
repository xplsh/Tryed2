FAQs

What are the requirements to join?

You must be 21+ (or legal age in your jurisdiction), pass our ID verification process, and have no active exclusivity contracts with other agencies or studios. We welcome performers at all experience levels, from newcomers to established professionals.

How do you ensure performer safety?

Our 5-point safety protocol includes: 1) Studio reputation vetting through our industry network, 2) Mandatory 48-hour testing for all productions, 3) On-set intimacy coordinators available upon request, 4) Clear boundaries documentation in all contracts, and 5) Anonymous reporting system for any concerns.

What's your commission structure?

We charge a transparent 15% commission on all bookings. Unlike some agencies, you see the full studio offer before accepting any project. Our commission covers continuous marketing, contract negotiation, payment processing, and 24/7 support services.

Can I work anonymously or with face blurred?

Absolutely. We specialize in masked/blurred performances and persona work. Over 30% of our talent maintain complete anonymity through stage names, body-only work, or digital masking. These preferences are documented in your profile and strictly enforced in all contracts.

How quickly can I expect bookings after joining?

Most performers receive their first suitable opportunity within 2-3 weeks of profile completion. Timing depends on your location, specialties, and current market demand. Our team will work with you to optimize your profile for faster placement.
